About The Website

Step into the fun world of the Drum Kit website, made by Maanya Sekhri. It's a cool place where you can play virtual drums by pressing keys on your keyboard or by clicking on the instrument icons.The website looks awesome, with big colorful buttons for different drum sounds. Whether you know a lot about music or just want to have some fun, this Drum Kit lets you make cool beats easily. It's a mix of modern style and classic drum sounds, and it's all about enjoying music. So, start drumming and have a great time making beats on this website created with a lot of love by Maanya Sekhri.


Creating this website involved the use of HTML,CSS and Javascript.
